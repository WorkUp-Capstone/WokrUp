Our Vision

We strive to fulfill a niche, but necessary purpose - helping inexperienced developers to grow their portfolio and confidence. At WorkUp, we allow for grads of career-accelerators to connect to real-projects by real clients that provide tangible in field experiences while they pursue a job in their newfound career.

Easy Start

Register an account and start posting projects, but it's proven that most people ignore empty profiles, so get more traffic to your projects for better turnaround by having a filled in profile.

Add Projects

You can immediately start to add projects and lets be real that is why your here! All you need to do is go to your profile and add a project, after filling out and submitting your project. It will now be listed to all available developers to look at and claim.

Options

As the owner of a project you get to choose who will ultimately work on your project. Inspect devs profiles and reach out or wait and have them come to you. Once a project is claimed you can either accept or decline the dev, once a dev is accepted it is expected that you connect to talk about expectations.

Closing a project

Once a dev has finished the project to their best abilities you will be notified that they are ready for you to review it, here you get the final say. You can either send it back notifying the dev that you intend to communicate the changes or mark it complete.

Your costs

Free work seems to good to be true, and it is; your cost is to ensure that you give a personal reference to the dev in regards to his work, stay on top of your profile project area to address all notifications of project status, and if satisfied spread WorkUp by word of mouth.

Easy Start

Register an account, set up a profile, and start searching for projects to work on. You can search for projects that include your skills or look at what other options the owner of a project has to offer.

Claim a project

Claim a project, when an owner accepts it you will see it in your profile, and can start immediately to connect with the owner to flesh out the details and establish expectations. This is all done via email.

Ready for review

Once you have a completed project submit for review and be notified if it need more work or if it is completed. If more needs to be done communicate with the owner to re-establish expectations and resubmit when appropriate. Otherwise, the cycle repeats.

Your benefits

You gain experience on real life projects, while solidifying and possibly learning new skills and technologies while establishing a networking connection to the owner. You will also gain a professional reference and portfolio work that will gain you another step closer to attaining a the experience needed to land the career you want!
